Know if you need an Optitian

Here are some guidelines to follow while taking the test

  1. Position yourself about 40 centimeters from the screen
  2. If you have glasses for distance vision or progressive lenses, have them on during the test
  3. You are rquired to take most tests twice, one for each eye. Cover your left or right eye apropriately without pressing. This will be the case unless indicated otherwise
